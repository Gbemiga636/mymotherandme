About the Experience

About the Experience

In a world that moves quickly, intentional moments between mothers and their children have never been more important.

Mothers and Their Sons & Daughters – The Legacy of Connection was created to offer a space where families can pause, connect, and grow together.

This elegant evening blends thoughtful activities, inspiring conversations, and meaningful experiences designed to strengthen family bonds and build confidence in the next generation.

Through shared moments of reflection, joy, and inspiration, families will leave with memories that extend far beyond one evening.

Our Vision

To cultivate meaningful connections between mothers and their children and create spaces where families can intentionally grow together.

Our Mission

To create a beautifully curated experience that inspires communication, confidence, and deeper relationships between mothers and their children.

Meet the Founder

Olubunmi Sonubi

Olubunmi Sonubi is the founder of BoomsyCreations, a luxury event design company known for creating elegant and meaningful experiences.

As a mother and creative visionary, Bunmi believes deeply in the importance of intentional family connection. Her passion for meaningful experiences led to the creation of Mothers and Their Sons & Daughters – The Legacy of Connection, an event designed to create space for families to pause, reflect, and grow together.

Through thoughtful design and purposeful programming, Bunmi is committed to creating moments that families will carry with them long after the evening ends.
